Spam musubi

Description

Sweet and savory, this recipe involves spam, sushi rice, sauce, and seaweed wrap!

Ingredients

  • Spam
  • Soy sauce
  • Sushi rice
  • Seaweed wrap
  • Corn starch
  • Dark brown sugar

Steps

  1. Cut spam
  2. Fry spam
  3. Whisk together soy, dark brown sugar, and corn starch when hot
  4. Shape rice and place spam on rice
  5. Wrap with seaweed
  6. Enjoy!
